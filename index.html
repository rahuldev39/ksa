About Mr. Kush Sharma

Advocate Kush Sharma has had an illustrious career as a member of the Bar, wherein he has represented clients at various fora, including the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India, Delhi High Court, The National Green Tribunal, The Debt Recovery Tribunals, The NCLT and many others.
