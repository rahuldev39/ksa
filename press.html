About Mr. Kush Sharma

Advocate Kush Sharma has had an illustrious career of over 12 years as a member of the Bar, wherein he has represented clients at various fora, including the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India, Delhi High Court, The National Green Tribunal, The Debt Recovery Tribunals, The NCLT and many others.

With over a decade of experience in the legal fraternity, he has earned a name for himself as a resolute lawyer who is determined to get the best outcome for his clients and thus is greatly respected by members of both, the Bench and the Bar.
