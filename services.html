Banking And Nbfcs And Financial Services

With the rise of Globalisation, the Banking and Finance sector in India has become increasingly complex resulting in reshaping and consolidation of the financial industry. Having worked closely with both lenders and borrowers for over a decade, our lawyers have a deep understanding of the wide ranging reforms and their impact on the market, allowing us to offer pragmatic solutions to our clients.

We have represented banks, financial institutions, non banking financial companies, borrowers and lenders. We represent all stakeholders in the financial sector and offer a comprehensive range of services including debt restructuring and refinancing, project finance, leveraged finance, distressed debt transactions, debt restructuring, acquisition finance, syndicated lending and structured products.

Family And Matrimonial Law

Our lawyers represent individuals in all aspects of Matrimonial and Family Law. We believe that personal attention is fundamental to these delicate matters. We focus on taking the time to know and understand the client’s needs and ensuring that our clients understand the process in advance which enables them to take informed decisions.

We handle a wide range of family law matters, helping families resolve their most personal and pressing issues including Divorce Matters, Maintenance Cases, Cases under Domestic Violence, Child Custody Matters, Restitution of Conjugal Rights, Dowry Cases, Rights of Hindu Women in Undivided Property, Cases pertaining to Rights of Muslim Women, Registration of Marriages, and Non-Resident Indian (NRI) Divorce Matters.

Intellectual Property Rights

We help clients protect their intellectual property by enforcing and defending intellectual property infringement actions, including trademark, copyright, and patent infringement claims, unfair competition lawsuits, trade secret and idea misappropriation claims in court. In conformance with our general approach to litigation, we seek the most viable cost effective solution consistent with the client’s business objectives.

With many years of government and industry experience, our Senior Partners understand the intricacies of litigation involving issues around Intellectual Property Rights. We have been handling various disputes related to IPR infringements which include representing our clients before various courts, authorities and forums in complex litigations issues, appeals and opposition proceedings.

Employment

We at KSA law offices LLP, are committed to providing strategic, high-level advice on crucial employment-related and compliance issues to guide our domestic and international clients through intricate regulatory and legal aspects of employment. We offer pragmatic advice to our clients on all aspects of employment and labour law on issues including the relevant applicable Indian labour laws, regulatory requirements mandated for employers, drafting and reviewing employment contracts and staff manuals, etc., in order to enable the clients to be legally compliant.

We represent Private companies, Public sector entities as well as individuals in their disputes relating to employment at the Central Administrative Tribunals, The High Courts and the Supreme Court of India. We deal with matters pertaining to unlawful termination, termination of employees either for cause or for reorganization of the business. Our lawyers are adept in creating tailor made solutions for our clients and protect their commercial interests.

Competition Law

Globalization and liberalization also leads to competition in the marketplace. Competition laws aim to control and curb monopolistic tendencies. India has enacted a new competition law called the Competition Act, 2002 ("Competition Act") in order to uphold healthy and fair competition in the market..

Our competition practice offers a broad range of services, including due diligence, drafting contracts and advising on non-compete clauses, providing innovative solutions and strategic advice on combination clearance and abuse of dominance. We provide assistance and representation to our clients in litigation before Competition Commission of India ("CCI"), the Competition Appellate Tribunal ("COMPAT") and other courts of judicature. We also advise on competition concerns in mergers and acquisitions, and for licensing and franchising agreements.

Environment Laws

We provide advice on environmental laws, rules and regulations, governmental incentives available to further environmental interests, including tax credits, grants and land use provisions. Our law firm advises its clients on regulatory compliances related to environment protection laws. Our attorneys have in depth knowledge and expertise on legal issues arising out of The Environment (Protection) Act, 1986, The Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974, The Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981, The Public Liability Insurance Act, 1991, Biological Diversity, 2002 etc.

We assist and provide advice to our clients on development of policies, procedures and systems, obtaining No Objection Certificates (NOCs) from Pollution Control Boards (both central and state) under Air and Water Pollution Acts, minimizing the risk of damage to their corporate reputation.

Our founding member Mr. Kush Sharma has been appearing before the National Green Tribunal since its establishment in 2010. Our Lawyers routinely represent Clients in environment related cases at various High Courts, The National Green Tribunal and the Supreme Court of India.

Election Laws

India has an asymmetric federal government, with elected officials at the federal, state and local levels. The Election Commission of India conducts elections at various level, given as under :

  • 1 General Elections (Lok sabha)
  • 2 Rajya Sabha Elections (upper House)
  • 3 State Assembly Elections
  • 4 Local Body Elections
  • 5 President Elections

We at KSA law offices LLP, have gained extensive experience in dealing with cases arising out of the Representation of People’s act, 1951. Our Lawyers have represented clients on various election disputes such as validity of elections, challenging Nomination, cancellation of Nomination and corrupt practices as per the election laws of India.
